Overview

Reimbursement Portal is an online portal where an employee can submit reimbursement claims related to relocation, medical, and office supplies, and the assigned manager can approve/reject the claims.

Functional Requirements

  • Three types of users: Admin, Manager, and Employee
  • Registration and login flow for Managers and Employees
  • Submission of reimbursement requests by Employees
  • Approval/rejection of reimbursement requests by Managers
  • Policy enforcement for reimbursement request categories and amounts
  • Ability for Admin to manage users and departments

Good to Have

  • Support for uploading multiple images for reimbursement requests

Out of Scope

  • Deletion of departments
